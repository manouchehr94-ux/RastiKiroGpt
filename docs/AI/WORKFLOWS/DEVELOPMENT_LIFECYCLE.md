# Development Lifecycle

1. Product owner reports need.
2. ChatGPT clarifies and writes request.
3. Kiro reads AI workspace and request.
4. Kiro audits current implementation.
5. ChatGPT reviews report.
6. Kiro implements approved fix.
7. Kiro runs tests.
8. Kiro commits.
9. Product owner pulls locally.
10. Product owner manually tests.
11. ChatGPT reviews result.
12. Feature is frozen or returned to backlog.
