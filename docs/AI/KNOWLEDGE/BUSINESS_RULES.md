# Business Rules

## Product

Rasti SaaS is a service dispatch platform.

Companies sell services.

Platform sells software.

---

## Customer Experience

Customer should feel they are dealing with the company.

Invoices display company information.

---

## Company

Each company controls its own service operations.

Each company may configure communication channels.

---

## Platform Owner

Platform owner controls templates, SaaS settings, commission policies, and platform-level configuration.
