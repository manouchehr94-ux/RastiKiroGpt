# Financial Rules

## Core Principle

Financial history must be auditable.

Do not edit historical ledger entries.

Corrections must create adjustment entries.

---

## Allocation vs Settlement

Allocation answers:

Who owns the money?

Settlement answers:

When and where the money is transferred.

These must remain separate concepts.

---

## Snapshots

Financial calculations must be frozen at the time of transaction.

Future policy changes must not change historical invoices.
